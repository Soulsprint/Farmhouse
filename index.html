About Our Farmhouse

A Sanctuary of Peace

Nestled in the heart of scenic countryside, our farmhouse offers a perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. Built in 1930 and lovingly restored, it combines rustic charm with modern comforts.

Our 50-acre property features organic gardens, fruit orchards, walking trails, and a small lake where guests can reconnect with nature and experience authentic farm life.

We're committed to sustainable practices and providing our guests with genuine farm-to-table experiences that nourish both body and soul.
